MISCELLANEOUS

Growing up in the medical community of Rochester, MN, I always dreamed of being a nurse. However, my dreams changed, as dreams often do, when I found that my interests and skills lay in creating appealing arrangements; whether that be arrangements of words in an essay, furniture in a room, or information on a poster.

An ability to stay organized, scrutinize details, communicate clearly, and arrange beautifully have long been strengths of mine, and continue to naturally saturate all that I do. Now as a student at North Dakota State University, I am learning the essential tools of marketing and design to promote people and organizations that seek to improve our world.
